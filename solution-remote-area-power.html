Remote Area Power Supply

Mobile PV-storage power for sites outside stable grid coverage.

This scenario applies to remote areas, island and border sites, weak-grid locations, and temporary project locations where power supply must be deployed, relocated, or expanded around site conditions.

Discuss This Scenario Back to Solutions

Scenario Need

Remote projects usually start with grid access uncertainty.

Customer pain points

  • Stable grid power may be unavailable, delayed, or too weak for project operation.
  • Fixed power stations are not ideal when equipment must move between sites.
  • Long-term diesel operation can increase fuel, maintenance, and inspection workload.
  • Project owners need clearer monitoring, backup, and expansion planning.

What must be confirmed

  • Load demand, operating hours, and critical load priority.
  • Grid condition: off-grid, weak-grid, or grid-connected backup.
  • Site access, relocation plan, and expected deployment conditions.
  • PV, battery, diesel generator, and monitoring requirements.
