Mobile PV-Storage Integrated Intelligent Microgrid System

A microgrid is a small-scale power generation and distribution system composed of distributed energy resources, energy storage systems, power conversion devices, loads, and monitoring and protection systems.

It can operate in parallel with the main grid for mutual power supply, and switch to island mode to independently supply power when the main grid fails, helping maintain electricity for critical loads.

The integration of microgrid systems and solar power container combines the autonomy and stability of microgrids with the portability and flexibility of foldable PV units for emergency power support, remote area power supply, industrial carbon reduction, and new-type power system construction.
