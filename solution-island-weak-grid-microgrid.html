Island & Weak-grid Microgrid

PV-storage microgrid power for island, border, and weak-grid sites.

This scenario applies to island, border, remote, and weak-grid locations where power supply needs PV, energy storage, backup integration, microgrid control, and project-specific expansion planning.

Discuss This ScenarioBack to Solutions

Scenario Need

Weak-grid projects need continuity, backup, and room to expand.

Customer pain points

  • Island, border, and remote sites may lack stable grid power.
  • Weak-grid environments require clearer backup and energy storage planning.
  • Long-term diesel operation can increase fuel and maintenance pressure.
  • Project owners need monitoring and protection for ongoing operation management.

What must be confirmed

  • Load demand, operating hours, and critical load priority.
  • Grid condition, outage frequency, and backup power requirement.
  • PV, battery, diesel generator, inverter, and monitoring requirements.
  • Site environment, installation access, and expected future expansion.
