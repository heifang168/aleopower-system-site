Construction Site Power

Movable temporary power for construction and changing project sites.

This scenario applies to construction sites, temporary power applications, maintenance and repair work, and project locations where equipment may need to move as the site changes.

Discuss This ScenarioBack to Solutions

Scenario Need

Construction site power needs to follow the project, not lock the site into one fixed layout.

Customer pain points

  • Grid access may be delayed or unavailable during early project phases.
  • Power equipment may need to move between work zones or future project sites.
  • Temporary diesel generation can raise fuel, service, and site management workload.
  • EPC and project owners need clearer power configuration before procurement.

What must be confirmed

  • Load demand by construction phase and operating hours.
  • Whether the site needs off-grid, weak-grid, or backup operation.
  • Mobility, relocation frequency, site access, and installation conditions.
  • PV, storage, diesel generator, and monitoring requirements.
