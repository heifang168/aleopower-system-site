Editorial Rules

Resources will be guides and comparisons, not fake news.

Resources content avoids unconfirmed certifications, customer names, delivery promises, factory scale, exact savings figures, or project countries unless they are supported by confirmed materials. FAQ remains on the homepage and will not be built as an independent page.

Content Template

Recommended article detail structure for future guides.

1. Problem Context

State the buyer's power supply challenge and application scenario.

2. System Explanation

Explain the product or solution using confirmed product categories and functions.

3. Selection Factors

List what the buyer should confirm: load, site, grid condition, mobility, and backup needs.

4. RFQ Checklist
