About ALEO Power

Power system solutions for mobile, off-grid, and weak-grid project sites.

ALEO Power is positioned as a power system solution provider, brand, and solution provider based in Guangzhou, China. The current website focuses on mobile solar power containers and PV-storage microgrid solutions for project buyers.

Company Profile

Focused on project-based power supply applications.

What We Provide

ALEO Power supports mobile solar power container, mobile hybrid power trailer, modular hybrid solar carport, off-grid system, and hybrid power system applications.

Who We Serve

The confirmed target buyers include EPC contractors, engineering companies, project owners, distributors, energy system integrators, mining and oilfield clients, emergency power teams, and infrastructure customers.

Where It Fits

The solutions are aimed at remote areas, temporary power sites, construction projects, emergency backup, island and weak-grid scenarios, mining, oil and gas, ports, and infrastructure applications.
