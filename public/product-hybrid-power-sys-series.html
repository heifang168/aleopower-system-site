Series Scope

Product and service directions extracted from B3106 PDF.

Project HOM

Solar Home System installed on house roofs, with flexible installation methods and sizes according to roof shape and nature.

Project CNI

Solar Commercial & Industrial System for reducing energy consumption and using idle top-level resources.

Project CPT

Solar Carport System combining PV modules with shed structure for shading, rain protection, charging, and clean energy generation.

Project MRO / SVC / EPC

Solar Microgrid System plus project design, complete set integration, on-site guidance, and EPC-oriented services.
